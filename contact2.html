Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I list my car for sale?

Listing your car is easy! Click on "Sell Your Car" in the navigation menu and follow our step-by-step process. You'll need to provide details about your vehicle, upload photos, set your price, and add your contact information.

Is there a fee to sell my car on AutoMarket?

No, listing your car on AutoMarket is completely free. We only charge a small success fee when your car sells through our platform. This helps us maintain and improve our services.

How do I contact a seller?

When you find a car you're interested in, click the "Contact" button on the listing. You'll be able to send a message directly to the seller through our secure messaging system.

What safety measures are in place?

We take safety seriously. All users are verified, and we provide secure messaging, transaction guidelines, and a rating system to ensure a safe experience for both buyers and sellers.

Can I schedule a test drive?

Yes! Once you've connected with a seller, you can arrange a test drive at a mutually convenient time and location. We recommend meeting in a public place and bringing a friend along for safety.
